## Writing Style for High-Quality Content

### What Google Values

1. **Expertise Demonstrated**: Show knowledge through examples, data, citations
2. **Original Voice**: Unique perspective, not generic information
3. **Helpful Intent**: Solve actual user problems
4. **Depth & Nuance**: Go deeper than surface-level explanations
5. **Accuracy**: Fact-based, citations where needed
6. **User Focus**: Written for the reader, not for SEO

### What Google Penalizes

- Thin content (300 words with little substance)
- Keyword stuffing
- Duplicate content from other sites
- No author information on expert content
- Misleading titles/descriptions
- Auto-generated content
- Unsourced claims
