# Google Publisher Network Quality Improvement Plan

## Executive Summary

OpenBalti has received a "Low value content" notice from Google, indicating the site doesn't meet minimum quality standards for the publisher network. This document outlines a comprehensive strategy to address Google's concerns and improve content quality across all pages.

**Current Issues:**
- Insufficient unique, high-quality content on some pages
- Thin content pages lacking depth and original insight
- Missing comprehensive guides and detailed resources
- Limited author expertise signals
- Insufficient internal linking and content organization

**Solution:** Expand content depth, add original research, create comprehensive guides, and improve E-A-T signals.

## Part 3: E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) Improvements

1. **Author Bios** - Add detailed author information:

   - Years of experience
   - Educational background
   - Language proficiency
   - Past work and projects

2. **Contributor Profiles** - Showcase community experts:
   - Native speakers featured
   - Linguists and academics
   - Cultural historians
   - Teacher profiles

3. **Expert Endorsements** - Add quotes and reviews from:
   - University linguistics departments
   - Language preservation organizations
   - Cultural institutions
   - Recognized scholars

### Authoritativeness Signals

1. **External Links** - Link to authoritative sources:
   - UNESCO language preservation resources
   - Academic publications on Balti
   - Linguistic research databases
   - Cultural organizations

2. **Citations** - Include proper citations for:
   - Historical facts
   - Statistical data
   - Linguistic information
   - Cultural details

3. **Fact Checking** - Verify all claims with sources

### Trustworthiness Signals

1. **About the Creator** - Detailed about page:
   - Mission and values
   - Team members
   - Transparency about funding
   - Contact information

2. **Privacy & Security** - Clear policies:
   - Privacy policy (already exists)
   - Terms of service (already exists)
   - Data handling practices
   - Security measures

3. **User Reviews & Testimonials** - Collect and display:
   - Success stories
   - User feedback
   - Community comments
   - Verified contributions

## Part 4: Technical Content Improvements

### Structural Enhancements

1. **Table of Contents** - Add to all long articles:
   ```html
   - Helps readers navigate
   - Improves SEO (featured snippets)
   - Shows content comprehensiveness
   ```

2. **Headers and Subheaders** - Use proper hierarchy:
   - H1: Main title (one per page)
   - H2: Major sections
   - H3: Subsections
   - H4: Details (max 3 levels deep)

3. **Content Blocks** - Break up text:
   - Bulleted lists
   - Numbered lists
   - Callout boxes
   - Info sidebars

### Media Enhancement

1. **Images** - Add high-quality images:
   - 1 image per 300-500 words
   - Descriptive alt text
   - Original images preferred
   - Infographics for data

2. **Videos** - Embed where relevant:
   - Pronunciation guides
   - Cultural demonstrations
   - Interview snippets
   - Learning tutorials

3. **Diagrams** - For complex topics:
   - Grammar structures
   - Dialect comparisons
   - Historical timelines
   - Family relationships

### Internal Linking Strategy

1. **Content Linking**:
   - Link blog posts to related learn pages
   - Link learn pages to dictionary entries
   - Create topic clusters
   - 3-5 internal links per page

2. **Context Anchors**:
   - Use descriptive anchor text
   - Avoid "click here"
   - Link to authoritative pages

## Part 6: Quality Metrics & Monitoring

### Content Quality Metrics

1. **Word Count**:
   - Main content pages: 2,000+ words
   - Blog posts: 1,500+ words
   - Guides: 2,500+ words
   - Dictionary entries: 150-300 words

2. **Content Freshness**:
   - New blog posts: Monthly minimum
   - Content updates: Quarterly
   - Broken link fixes: Monthly
   - Stats updates: Quarterly

3. **Engagement Metrics**:
   - Average time on page: 2+ minutes
   - Scroll depth: 80%+
   - Internal click-through: 15%+
   - Return visitor rate: 25%+

### Google Search Console Monitoring

1. **Impressions & CTR**:
   - Track trending keywords
   - Monitor ranking improvements
   - Watch for new opportunities
   - Identify thin content gaps

2. **Coverage Issues**:
   - Fix excluded URLs
   - Resolve crawl errors
   - Update indexation status
   - Remove noindex where appropriate

3. **Core Web Vitals**:
   - Maintain LCP < 2.5s
   - Keep CLS < 0.1
   - Monitor INP < 200ms

### Quarterly Reviews

1. **Content Performance**:
   - Which pages drive traffic
   - Which convert best
   - Which need improvement
   - Content gap analysis

2. **SEO Progress**:
   - Ranking improvements
   - Traffic growth
   - Keyword expansion
   - Competitive analysis

3. **Quality Assessment**:
   - E-A-T signal strength
   - Peer comparison
   - User feedback
   - Expert review
