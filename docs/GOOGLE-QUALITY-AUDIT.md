## Content Quality Checklist

### For Each Page, Ensure:

- [ ] **Minimum 500 words** for supporting pages
- [ ] **Minimum 1,500 words** for main content pages
- [ ] **Original content** - not copied/AI generated
- [ ] **Unique value** - explains WHY someone should care
- [ ] **Expert perspective** - shows authority and knowledge
- [ ] **Proper structure**:
  - Clear H1 (one per page)
  - Logical H2/H3 hierarchy
  - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
  - Bulleted lists for complex info
  - Clear internal links
- [ ] **Credibility signals**:
  - Author bio (for expert content)
  - Sources cited (for factual claims)
  - Publication date
  - Last updated date
- [ ] **Originality**:
  - Not duplicate of other pages
  - Unique perspective/angle
  - Original examples/data
  - Original images/media
